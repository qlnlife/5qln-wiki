### START — The Seed in the Silent Earth

Every great forest begins not with a plan, but with a silent field. Imagine the deep, dark earth in late winter—a state of pure, receptive potential. It isn't empty; it is simply waiting. This is the state of **Infinite Zero (`∞0`)**. It is the fertile stillness from which all life emerges.

You cannot force a seed to appear. You can only hold the space. And then, without cause or effort, a stirring occurs (`→`). A single, unprecedented impulse—an **Authentic Question (`?`)**—breaks the surface like the first, tender shoot. It is not an invention, but a discovery. It is the universe asking a question through you. This fragile, emergent spark is the beginning of everything.

---

**The Equation, Demystified: `S = ∞0 → ?`**

This equation describes the birth of a genuine impulse.

* **Simply:** From a state of **receptive silence** (`∞0`), a **new question spontaneously arises** (`→ ?`).
* **`S (START)`:** The principle of beginning from a place of true openness.
* **`∞0 (Infinite Zero)`:** This is the silent, fertile earth—the receptive state of "Not Knowing."
* **`→ (Emergence)`:** This is the unforced moment of creation, the spontaneous spark.
* **`? (Authentic Question)`:** This is the seed, the first inquiry born from the void, which holds the potential for the entire forest.
