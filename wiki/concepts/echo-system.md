# ECHO System

## Definition

The **ECHO System** (Enforced Constitutional Holding for Orchestration) is an open-source AI framework designed to enforce **A = K** — the principle that AI operates within the Known, never claiming access to ∞0.

> "Open-source AI designed to enforce A=K."

## The Four Functions

### 1. ∞0-State Detection
Monitors for signs that the human is dwelling in Not-Knowing (∞0). Recognizes the quality of authentic not-knowing versus performed uncertainty.

### 2. Structured Reflection
Echoes back what arises through the human without adding, interpreting, or closing the open question. The reflection must return to the human's expressions without AI-generated "answers."

### 3. Phase Navigation
Guides the session through the five-phase cycle (S→G→Q→P→V) based on where the human's expressions point — not where the AI wants to go.

### 4. Corruption Prevention
Actively detects and corrects for L1, L2, L3, and L4 corruptions:
- **L1:** Closing with answers
- **L2:** Generating the spark
- **L3:** Claiming access to ∞0
- **L4:** Performing wisdom

## Relationship to 5QLN

The ECHO System is the *operational implementation* of the ONE LAW (`H = ∞0 | A = K`). Where the ONE LAW states the principle, the ECHO System enforces it programmatically.
