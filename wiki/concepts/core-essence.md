### **GROWTH — The Unfolding of the Inner Pattern**
Once the seed has sprouted, it begins to grow. It does not need to invent what it is. Its core identity—its fundamental "treeness," its **Core Essence (`α`)**—is already present. This inner code remains unchanged (`≡`) throughout its life.
The pattern then reveals itself in a symphony of **Self-Similar Expressions (`{α'}`)**. The way the roots branch mirrors the way the limbs reach for the sky. The veins in a single leaf echo the shape of the entire tree. Each part is different, yet each is a perfect expression of the same core identity. Growth is not an act of addition, but a magnificent, coherent unfolding of a pattern that was always there.
* * *
**The Equation, Demystified:`G = α ≡ {α'}`**
This equation describes how a core identity expresses itself everywhere.
  * **Simply:** A single, **unchanging essence** (`α`) maintains its **identity** (`≡`) across **many different forms** (`{α'}`).
  * **`G (GROWTH)`:** The principle of coherent, natural unfolding.
  * **`α (Core Essence)`:** The unchanging DNA of the inquiry, its "treeness."
  * **`≡ (Identity Preservation)`:** The symbol that this core essence remains identical and true.
  * **`{α'} (Self-Similar Expressions)`:** The various manifestations—the roots, the leaves, the branches—that all share the same essential pattern.
