# Self-Nature (φ)

The tree's authentic, unique identity as it has grown — what seeks alignment with the universal in the Quality phase.

## Definition

In `Q = φ ⋂ Ω`, φ is the individual's authentic nature — shaped by the Growth phase's unfolding of [[core-essence]], now seeking resonance with [[universal-potential]].

φ is not static identity but the living shape of what has grown from the [[authentic-question]]. It is "unique" — no two trees are the same — yet it seeks connection, not isolation.

## Relationship to Core Essence

φ in Quality is what α (from Growth) has become through unfolding. α is the seed's DNA; φ is the young tree's actual shape. They are related but not identical — φ carries the marks of real growth in real conditions.
