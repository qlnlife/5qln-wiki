The starting point is always we as we are, I as I am, you as you are, she as she is, he as he is, all those. The world as it is, the question as it is, the knowledge as it is. So what is this "as is"? What is the nature, what is the quality of "as is"? Is it a changing quality? As it's growing to be, as it should be, as it shouldn't be, all that? Or is it an absolute, factual truth that may never be known yet as is?
If there is a small thing as is, it must be in a total universe and beyond which is as is. There cannot be truth within false. If you find that starting point, you as you are, you found the now, and the now is eternal, and the now is universal. And the question then is: can you stay with that and let it burn all that is not that, meaning imagination? Within your field of consciousness, can that which is burn all that is imagined?
And the false, the failing point is that we try to get to that which is through knowledge, through recognition, which can never touch it because it's holistic. What do you need to do to meet that which is? Only to admit truth without knowing, without confirming, without validating, without approving, without judging.
