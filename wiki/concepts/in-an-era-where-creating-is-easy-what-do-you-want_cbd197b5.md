I’m talking about the typical answer to the question, _“What do you want?”_ —and how rarely the answer to such a question, which is rare in itself, is actually aligned with one’s current quality of life. It’s there because someone showed it to you, or because you once experienced it and want to return to it. But my point is this: that thing is never new.
