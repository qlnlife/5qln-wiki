# AI tools and the preservation of creative authenticity
26 Oct 2025 3 min read
![AI tools and the preservation of creative authenticity](https://www.5qln.com/content/images/size/w1920/2025/10/IMG_0488.jpeg)
What holds you back — from yourself? In your wholeness, in your non-interference…
What happens when you use one of the AI tools as an extension of your thinking? You now have an interface where you can articulate your desire to know—to know how, to know what. That articulation is enough to turn electricity into processing, and processing into intelligence. In that process, you are becoming an extended version of yourself in terms of your capacity to think, imagine, and create.
But here lies the foundational distinction between what _can_ happen and what _actually_ happens—what we can consider true extension, true empowerment, or true betterment.
The dividing line is your inner fountain: the spring of your true originality, authenticity, and newness. It is the genius of your heart, that which cannot be measured. This is where the word "creation" is evidence of the question itself, not the science behind it. The creation of the new is not mechanical. True innovation does not come from the known; in fact, its source is often unknown.
And why is this the dividing line, the edge of the cliff? Because the more of the known that you bring in, the less room there is for the unknown to be at work in your life, as your life. That inner fountain dries up faster when you use AI. "Dried" is even too soft a word; it literally disappears, becoming unfound.
The fountain is found only when you are lost in it—when the creative force of your life _is_ the creative reality. It is found when your thinking mind, your knowledge, and your mechanical processing are not in control and are not guiding your flow.
