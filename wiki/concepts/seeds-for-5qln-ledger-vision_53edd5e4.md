## **A Sundial for the Post-ASI Economy**
The vision is to create **an operating system for human creative sovereignty in the age of artificial superintelligence** —a decentralized platform where humans retain their irreducible role as sources of authentic novelty while AI becomes a powerful partner in manifesting that creativity.
### **The Core Insight**
As ASI achieves mastery over the **Domain of the Known** —processing, recombining, and optimizing all accumulated human knowledge—the fundamental source of value shifts. In a post-scarcity world where AI can replicate almost anything, **the only irreducible value is what AI cannot create: authentic novelty from the Domain of the Unknown**.
Only humans can originate from **Infinite Zero (∞0)** —the state of radical openness and "Not Knowing" from which genuine creativity emerges. AI cannot truly "not know"; it can only simulate lack of data.
### **The Symbiotic Partnership**
The vision establishes a new human-AI relationship:
  * **Humans as "Vessels of the Unknown"** : Sourcing authentic questions, intuition, and creative guidance from ∞0
  * **AI as "Echo of the Known"** : Structuring, manifesting, and propagating human-sourced novelty within the vast domain of existing knowledge


This is not replacement—it's profound **complementarity**.
