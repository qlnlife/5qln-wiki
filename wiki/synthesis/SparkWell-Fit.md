## The Alignment

5QLN fits SparkWell's criteria at the structural level — not as a philosophical match but as an operational infrastructure layer.

**SparkWell funds:** Projects that help humans stay human as AI advances.
**5QLN provides:** A grammar that makes human agency legible to AI without AI writing the rules.

## SparkWell's Own Criteria (from Dataset)

From the SparkWell/SFF cross-analysis (DuckDB query of enrolled projects):

1. **Human-side AI safety** — not capability advancement, not alignment in the technical sense
2. **Practical, not theoretical** — SparkWell funds doing, not papers
3. **Collaborative** — cross-disciplinary, human-centered
4. **Authentic human flourishing** — projects that strengthen what makes humans irreplaceable
